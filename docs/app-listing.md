### Migrate to Shopify in hours, not weeks

Moving your store to Shopify shouldn't cost thousands of dollars or take months. StoreMigrator Pro gives you the same data transfer capabilities that migration agencies charge $2,000–$5,000 for, at a fraction of the price — and you stay in full control.

### Built for serious merchants

Whether you're moving a 50-product shop off Etsy or a 25,000-SKU catalog off Magento, StoreMigrator Pro handles it. We've optimized for real migration scenarios: messy product data, variant-heavy catalogs, customer records with years of order history, and SEO-critical URL structures that can make or break your organic traffic after launch.

### What you can migrate

- **Products:** Titles, descriptions, variants, prices, SKUs, inventory, images, vendor, product type, tags

- **Metafields:** Custom attributes and metadata preserved across the migration

WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Magento 1 & 2, Squarespace, Wix, Etsy, PrestaShop, OpenCart, Volusion, Shift4Shop, Ecwid, Weebly, and many more. Don't see yours? Generic CSV and JSON imports work with any platform that lets you export your data.

## Review Notes (message to Shopify reviewers)

Hello Shopify review team,

StoreMigrator Pro is a store migration app that helps merchants import their existing catalog, customer, and order data into Shopify from 140+ platforms.

**How to test:**

1. Install the app on a development store
2. You'll see a dashboard with a "Start New Migration" button
3. Click through: select source platform (try "Generic CSV"), select entity type, upload the sample CSV provided at `/docs/sample-products.csv` in our repo
4. Start the migration — you'll see progress tracking on the job detail page
5. Free tier allows 50 items; larger imports prompt the billing flow
6. For billing, use Shopify's billing API test mode — charges won't post in development

**Test credentials not required** — app is self-serve from install.

**Webhooks:** All 5 mandatory webhooks are implemented (app/uninstalled, app/scopes_update, customers/data_request, customers/redact, shop/redact).

**Data handling:** We do not persist customer PII. Migration source files are processed in-memory and discarded after import.

Thanks for reviewing!
